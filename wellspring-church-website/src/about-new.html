Our Values

The core principles that guide everything we do at Wellspring Church

Who We Are

At Wellspring Church, we are committed to Growing, Equipping and Sending Disciples of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to create a community where people can experience God's love, grow in their faith, and make a difference in the world.

Our Mission

To grow, equip, and send disciples of Jesus Christ who will transform their communities and the world through the power of God's love.

Our Vision

To be a church where everyone can find their place, grow in their relationship with Jesus, and discover their purpose in God's kingdom.

Our Heart

We believe that the local church is God's hope for the world. We are passionate about creating an environment where people can encounter Jesus, experience authentic community, and be equipped to make a difference in their world.
