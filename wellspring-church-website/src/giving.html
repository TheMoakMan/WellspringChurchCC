GIVING

Partnering with God to transform lives and communities

Why We Give

At Wellspring Church, we believe that giving is an act of worship and a way to partner with God in His work. Your generosity helps us fulfill our mission of Growing, Equipping, and Sending Disciples of Jesus.

Worship Through Giving

Giving is an expression of our love and gratitude to God for all He has done for us. It's a way to honor Him with the resources He has entrusted to us.

2 Corinthians 9:7

Ministry Support

Your tithes and offerings directly support our ministries, missions, and the daily operations that help us serve our community and beyond.

Philippians 4:19

Kingdom Impact

Together, we're making a difference in lives - locally through outreach and globally through missions. Every gift, large or small, makes an impact.

Matthew 28:19-20

What God Says About Giving

Our approach to giving is grounded in Biblical principles and motivated by love, not obligation.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

- 2 Corinthians 9:7

We believe that giving should be a joyful expression of our faith, not a burden. Whether you're able to give a little or a lot, what matters most is the heart behind the gift. God sees and blesses every act of generosity, no matter the amount.

Remember: It's not about the amount - it's about the heart
